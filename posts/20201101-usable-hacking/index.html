Usable Hacking

Sunday, November 1, 2020

What if there was an all in one hacking software that was more accessible?

You would specify your task/goal (root access, adversarial obfuscation, DoS), and it would provide you with the highest probability steps and tools to achieve your goal.

  • Network mappings, cookies, keylogging, packets, man in middle
  • Social engineering AI phishing, spoofing, vishing, mass email
  • Disinformation AI text generation, misinformation articles, commenting, social media posting, websites
  • Vulnerabilities, exploits, trojans, (detect target OS, version, and the associated CVEs)
  • Password cracking online, offline, entropy based, AI based, dictionary
  • Website security
